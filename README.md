# Corporate Investment Preference Analysis & Executive Dashboard

An end-to-end data aggregation, demographic profiling, and visualization project completed during my Data Visualization Virtual Internship at **Cognifyz Technologies**. This project processes raw financial survey responses from 40 unique participants to isolate key trends behind how demographics, information channels, and primary motivations influence asset selection.

---

## 📂 Project Structure

The project workbooks and supporting documentation are organized as follows:
*   **`level_1_task_1.xlsx`** – Baseline data structuring and introductory metric calculations.
*   **`level_2_task.xlsx`** – Multi-variable breakdowns, trend mapping, and intermediate aggregations.
*   **`level_3_tasks.xlsx`** – Advanced relational breakdowns, distribution structuring, and scatter plot matrices.
*   **`level_3_tasks_dashboard.xlsx`** – The final consolidated production workbook containing the integrated executive dashboard tab.
*   **`Pictures/`** – High-resolution snapshot exports of specific pivot breakdowns, task states, and final dashboard configurations.

## 📊 Core Data Metrics & Domain Insights

Based on the survey population ($N = 40$), the project surfaces clear, actionable financial indicators across four distinct operational pillars:

### 1. Demographic Distribution
*   **Gender Split:** The survey data comprises **25 Male** participants and **15 Female** participants, forming a structured dataset to analyze behavioral differences across genders.

### 2. Asset Allocation & Decision Drivers
*   **Investment Avenue Preferences:** **Mutual Funds** emerged as the dominant financial vehicle with **18 selections**, followed by **Equity (10)**, **Fixed Deposits (9)**, and **Public Provident Funds (3)**.
*   **Core Investment Factors:** When selecting investment channels, **Returns** served as the absolute primary driver (valued heavily by 25 out of 40 respondents), with **Risk assessment** being the second most critical criterion at 14 responses. 

### 3. Financial Motivations
*   **Savings Objectives:** Capital growth is the leading priority among investors, with **65% focusing purely on Capital Appreciation** and **28% prioritizing general Growth**, while only **7% seek direct Income**.

### 4. Information Media Impact
*   **Media Channels:** Investors lean most heavily on **Financial Consultants (16)** and **Newspapers & Magazines (14)** to guide their wealth allocation, outperforming modern internet search engines and television channels as primary advisory sources.

## 🛠️ Implementation & Technical Features

*   **Pivot Table Architecture:** Transformed raw row-level survey data rows into multidimensional tables to derive cross-tabulated insights (e.g., matching Investment Factors directly against Preferred Avenues).
*   **Advanced Visualizations:** Deployed specific chart formats matching data shapes—using **Pie Charts** for fractional distributions, **Stacked/Grouped Column Bars** for factor analysis, and **Horizontal Bar Charts** for categorical rankings.
*   **Unified Dashboard Canvas:** Extracted individual charts from siloed sheets into a primary visual interface (`dashboard`) optimizing information scannability.
*   **Interactive Controls (Slicers):** Implemented contextual data slicers with cross-connected report channels, enabling a single click to dynamically filter metrics across the entire layout simultaneously.
